The Beginning

Where It All Started

Dead Stock was born out of a love for street culture, rare pieces, and the idea that fashion doesn't have to be fast. We started small — hunting thrift stores, flea markets and forgotten wardrobes for gems that deserved a second life.

What started as a passion became a movement. Every piece we carry has a story, a character, a soul. We don't do trends — we do timeless.

Based in Sholapur, Maharashtra, we're bringing the thrift culture to India — one rare find at a time.
