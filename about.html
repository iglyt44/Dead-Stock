THE VAULT STORY

Dead Stock was born from the frustration of seeing good pieces rot in closets and landfills. We hunt, clean, and revive rare vintage streetwear, watches, jewelry and accessories that deserve a second run on the streets.

OUR MISSION

We believe in giving dead stock new life. Every piece you see here has a story — from the 90s to early 2010s. We rescue them before they disappear forever.

THE PROCESS

Sourcing → Cleaning & Repair → Photography → Curation. Only the pieces that pass our "drip test" make it into The Vault.

FOR THE CULTURE

This isn’t fast fashion. This is culture preservation. Supporting real style over trends.
